Reduce the risk of Alzheimer’s Disease


Follow the plans regularly!

Food plays a major role in treating a lot of diseases today, So it is highly advisable to eat a balanced diet,containing all kinds of nutrients to get a healthy system. Alzheimer’s is a complicated disease in which a person starts losing their concentration and memory. Alzheimer’s disease requires treatment and proper medical attention to get cured. With the medical aid, patients are advised to have a proper diet, that contains a lot of brain-boosting food items. These food items contain certain nutrients which might help in delaying the symptoms of the Alzheimer's disease and improve the quality of life.

Nutritious Diet Plan


Fatty fish

Powerhouse of omega-3 fatty acids are the Fatty fishes.It contains various health benefits. To include in your daily diet, The best food items for people with Alzheimer's Disease is oily fish like salmon,sardines and trout,So try to include those items in your daily diet.Our brain is made up of fatty cells that mainly contains omega-3,So,it will improve your brain functioning and boost your memory power.


Nuts

Nuts contains Vitamins and Minerals.They are highly essential for your brain health.Nuts like walnuts and almonds play major role in boosting memory power,Vitamin E in walnut is also plays important role in reducing the risk of Alzheimer's Disease.Try to include 2-5 walnuts in your daily diet to improves cognitive health. Omega-3 fatty acids in nuts are highly recommended for the people who want to improve memory power.


Turmeric

Turmeric is a widely used spice, especially in Ayurveda. It has a various health benefits and is used in various ways. Turmeric has anti-oxidant and anti-inflammatory property.Due to this property it is used in the treatment of brain impairments. Turmeric helps in reducing inflammation of the brain cells and eases depression.Helps in the growth of new brain cells due to the compunds present in the turmeric.


Avocados

Avocados are a powerhouse of healthy monounsaturated fatty acids,vitamin K,potassium.They plays a major role in improving and maintaining good brain health. These fruits help in improving the cognitive function of the brain. It boosts your concentration power and memory.


Pumpkin seeds

Pumpkin seeds plays important role in relieving your stress levels.Thwy helps in improving your mood.Pumpkin seeds are high in magnesium, copper, zinc. The deficiency of any of these minerals is related to Alzheimer's disease. These minerals are needed for improving and boosting brain functions.
